the lesson we carry

The Lesson We'll Always Remember

Life is like boiling water.

When salt is placed into boiling water, it dissolves quietly.

It blends in.

When a raw egg is placed into boiling water, it becomes hard inside.

Pressure changes it.

But when coffee is placed into boiling water,
it doesn't let the water change it —
it changes the water.

You taught us to be the coffee.
